- **Drive Vectors radar chart**: dynamic axis count, radial gradient fill
- **SDG Alignment**: official UN SDG icons with resonance rings
- **Identity Timeline**: reversed order, gradient connector lines
- **Strengths + Watch Outs**: 2-column grid
- **Q&A panel**: auth-gated, Enter to send
